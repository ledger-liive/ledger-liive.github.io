How do I connect my Ledger to my desktop?

After successfully installing Ledger Live on your device, you then need to connect Ledger Live to your hardware wallet. For this, you can check out the steps that are listed below. These steps can be applied to Ledger Stax, Ledger Nano X, and Ledger Nano S Plus devices.

  • Use the given USB cable to connect the Ledger wallet to your desktop
  • Further, you should press the left or right buttons to navigate the interface
  • Press the two buttons at the same time to select "Set up as new device"
  • Then, you must choose a PIN to encrypt your wallet by pressing the two buttons again
  • Confirm the PIN code by entering it one more time
  • Right after this, you will be asked to note down the words of your Secret Recovery Phrase one by one
  • After noting it safely, you can then confirm the seed phrase
  • Follow the on-screen steps and you are good to go
